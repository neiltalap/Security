# Digital Forensics Ethics

Endpoint digital forensics involves analyzing evidence from devices to investigate crime. This field requires strict adherence to ethical standards.

## Ethical Requirements
- **Privacy:** Obtaining legal warrants and analyzing only relevant data (data minimization).
- **Integrity:** Maintaining the chain of custody and documenting analysis thoroughly.
- **Fairness:** Avoiding bias based on race, gender, or religion.
- **Responsible Disclosure:** Keeping findings confidential and disclosing vulnerabilities responsibly.
